STEM

STEM is an interdisciplinary approach in the Philippine education system that stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

SCIENCE

Science is the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment. it aims to foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and an understanding of scientific concepts, preparing students for higher education and careers in scientific and technological fields.

TECHNOLOGY

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry. it aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge to develop, utilize, and manage technological tools and innovations, preparing them for modern careers and problem-solving in a technologically-driven world.

ENGINEERING

Engineering is the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, and systems. it aims to develop students' analytical and technical skills, fostering innovation and preparing them for careers in various engineering fields to address real-world challenges.

MATHEMATICS

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, and the logical reasoning behind them. it aims to develop students' problem-solving and analytical skills, providing a strong foundation for understanding and applying mathematical concepts in various scientific and technological fields.

Features

This website aids STEM students at the University of the Cordilleras in their academic journey with three main features:
Archives, Community, and Quizzes.

Archives

The archive functions as a research repository, aiding STEM students in the development of their research papers and references. This feature ensures easy access to a wealth of information, facilitating thorough and efficient research. By providing a comprehensive and organized database, the archive supports academic excellence and innovation.

Community

The Community feature offers previews of upcoming STEM Society events. It also highlights activities from other organizations at the University of the Cordilleras. This ensures students stay informed about the latest events and opportunities within the university community. Engaging with these events fosters a collaborative and enriching academic experience.

Quizzes

The Quizzes feature offers valuable practice for STEM students, covering a wide range of topics from science to mathematics. This tool is specifically designed to support students in achieving their academic goals. By offering targeted practice and detailed feedback, that helps their understanding and proficiency in various STEM subjects.
